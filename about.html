Eric N. Cohen & Associates represents venture capital, small to middle-market companies, start-ups and entrepreneurs in all areas of corporate, transactional and real estate work. We pride ourselves on delivering highly skilled legal counsel and professional workproduct on par with New York’s finest firms.

The firm advises on and deals with the full range of business transactions, including mergers and acquisitions, structuring joint ventures, financings, licensing and other intellectual property matters, real estate, leasing and executive compensation.

We place a priority on the client relationship and view the transaction from our client’s perspective – understanding the economics behind the deal and always seeking to achieve our client’s objectives. Our clients include private equity and venture capital funds, internet and technology businesses, firms in the manufacturing and construction industry, fashion, apparel and consumer goods companies, restaurants, and companies engaged in the entertainment business.

Our network of associates and practitioners have legal and business expertise across various fields to meet the needs of any particular transaction, including employee relations, commercial and general tort litigation, bankruptcy and intellectual property.
