ERIC N. COHEN & ASSOCIATES, PLLC

AREAS OF PRACTICE

Mergers & Acquisitions
We specialize in structuring, negotiating, and closing transactions for buyers, sellers, and related parties -- including private equity groups and venture capital firms. We represent clients in private M&A transactions, ranging from small to multi-million-dollar transactions and have handled a full spectrum of acquisitions and dispositions, including stock and asset purchases, tender offers, venture capital investments, management buy-outs, mergers and restructurings.

Contract Drafting
The Firm negotiates and drafts agreements of all kinds, including licensing, manufacturing, employment, consulting, joint venture, confidentiality and non-disclosure agreements. Our contract experience spans a wide range of commercial fields, including businesses in the fashion, apparel and related consumer goods fields, entertainment and technology companies, and manufacturing concerns.

Corporate Entity Formation
The Firm has helped clients to form and launch new companies, whether corporations, partnerships, LLCs, S-corps or not-for-profit organizations. We provide hands-on service to new companies seeking to establish themselves, tailoring key documents to meet our clients' specific needs.

Real Estate
The Firm provides legal services in the purchase and sale of real estate, commercial leasing, commercial and residential development; corporate, securities and tax matters related to real estate ownership; landlord and tenant matters; eminent domain; environmental law; and zoning.

Intellectual Property
The Firm provides a broad range of legal representation in patent, trademark and copyright, matters. We have represented software and technology firms in contract matters concerning the licensing and protection of proprietary intellectual property rights.

Fashion Industry
We represent several companies in the textile and apparel industry, and provide expertise in intellectual property, licensing and contract negotiation. The firm offers these areas, as well as a full understanding of the business issues faced by fashion companies in today's global marketplace.

Entertainment
The Firm has handled a variety of transactional entertainment matters in the film, television and music industries complemented by its experience in trademark and copyright. We have represented film companies, television and motion picture executives as well as recording artists, songwriters and producers.
