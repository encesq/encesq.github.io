ERIC N. COHEN & ASSOCIATES, PLLC

ABOUT US

Eric N. Cohen & Associates, PLLC represents small to middle-market companies, private equity and entrepreneurs in all areas of corporate, transactional and real estate work. We pride ourselves in delivering highly-skilled legal counsel and professional workproduct on par with New York's finest firms. However, as a small firm, we can offer a value alternative to clients seeking first-class legal services at reasonable rates.

We maintain a network of close relationships with practitioners having legal and business expertise in various fields to meet the needs of any particular transaction, including tax structuring and advice, employee relations, bankruptcy and litigation. The Firm is located within the trademark and real estate law firm McLaughlin & Wilshinsky, P.C. and has access to its partners and resources. This structure allows Eric N. Cohen & Associates to provide a value alternative to clients seeking sophisticated legal services across a broad range of practice areas.

We place a priority on the client relationship. We expect to work collaboratively with the client and view the transaction from the client's perspective -- understanding the economics behind the deal and always seeking to achieve the client's objectives. Eric N. Cohen is founding partner and gained his legal experience practicing at the leading New York firms Kramer, Levin, Naftalis & Frankel and Mayer, Brown, Rowe & Maw. Mr. Cohen graduated Magna Cum Laude from the Benjamin N. Cardozo School of Law and received his undergraduate degree from the University of Michigan at Ann Arbor.

Some of Mr. Cohen's corporate/business accomplishments at the Firm and in his prior practice include:

  • Negotiating and closing a $2 million dollar Series A Preferred Stock venture capital investment in a wireless communications product developer.

  • Representing a juniorswear licensee in connection with several apparel industry licensing deals, covering celebrity fashion marks and joint venture deal terms.

  • Closing a $20 million private sale of a closely-held novelty products wholesaler of its business (including its foreign subsidiaries) to a middle market venture capital fund.

  • Representing a U.S. call services company in joint venture with a Philippines business group to form and operate an international call center in the Philippines.

  • Representing New York Marina business in connection with its plans sell a sizable parcel of real estate for redevelopment.

  • Representing a creative design company in developing its form consulting and services agreements.
