FEES

The Firm's basic fee for service compares favorably with the "going rate" for experienced corporate attorneys in New York. Our charges are based primarily on the amount of time devoted to a particular client's matter. However, consistent with our client-oriented philosophy, we are always mindful of containing costs, working efficiently and obtaining the best legal and financial results for our clients.

Further, as a smaller firm we can be flexible. In appropriate cases, we have worked on a special fee arrangement or have created fee reduction techniques to meet a client's particular needs. We have agreed, for example, to flat fees for particular phases of work on a transaction in suitable cases.
