ERIC N. COHEN & ASSOCIATES, PLLC

Eric N. Cohen & Associates, PLLC provides highly-skilled legal counsel in commercial, corporate and real estate matters. We are a solutions oriented firm, focused on providing legal service to small to middle-market companies, private equity and entrepreneurs at a level of sophistication and care comparable to New York's finest firms.

The Firm has assisted businesses in all phases of operation, from initial formation and capital raising, through commercial agreements and financings, to the purchase or sale of the business.

We take pride in building close relationships with our clients, providing cost-effective representation, with excellent service and quickest response times.
