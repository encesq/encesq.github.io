MERGERS & ACQUISITIONS
The Firm advises on structuring, negotiating, and closing transactions for buyers, sellers, and related parties – including private equity groups and venture capital firms. We represent clients in private M&A transactions, ranging from small to multi-milliondollar transactions and have handled a full spectrum of acquisitions and dispositions, including stock and asset purchases, tender offers, venture capital investments, management buyouts, mergers and restructurings.

REAL ESTATE
We provide legal services in the purchase and sale of real estate, commercial leasing, commercial and residential development; corporate and securities matters related to real estate ownership; and landlord and tenant matters.

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
The Firm provides a broad range of legal representation in patent, trademark and copyright, matters. We have represented software and technology firms in contract matters concerning the licensing and protection of proprietary intellectual property rights.

CORPORATE ENTITY FORMATION
The Firm has helped clients to form and launch new companies, whether corporations, partnerships, LLCs, S-corps or not-for-profit organizations. We provide hands-on service to new companies seeking to establish themselves, tailoring key documents to meet our clients’ specific needs.

CONTRACT DRAFTING
We negotiate and draft agreements of all kinds, including licensing, manufacturing, employment, consulting, joint venture, confidentiality and non-disclosure agreements. Our contract experience spans a wide range of commercial fields, including businesses in the fashion, apparel and related consumer goods fields, entertainment and technology companies, and manufacturing concerns.

CIVIL LITIGATION
We have also handled numbers of civil litigation matters for our clients, including partnership disputes, real estate litigation and other commercial disputes. We have also taken on select personal injury matters, including vehicle injury and medical malpractice matters, each having resulted in a favorable settlement for our clients.

FASHION INDUSTRY
We represent several companies in the textile and apparel industry, and provide expertise in intellectual property, licensing and contract negotiation. The firm offers these areas, as well as a full understanding of the business issues faced by fashion companies in today’s global marketplace.

ENTERTAINMENT
The Firm has handled a variety of transactional entertainment matters in the film, television and music industries complemented by experience in trademark and copyright law. We have represented film companies, television and motion picture executives as well as recording artists, songwriters and producers.

RESTAURANT LAW
The Firm represents owners and entrepreneurs in the New York restaurant and bar industry. We counsel our clients with respect to starting the business, obtaining investment capital, negotiating commercial leases, applying for liquor licenses, and drafting and negotiating all agreements for the purchase and sale of restaurants.
